# Scrollingo Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Scrollingo ("we", "our", or "us") provides a mobile application designed to help users reduce distracting app usage and practice language learning.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Scrollingo may access, how it is used, and what choices users have.

## 1. Information We Access

Depending on the features you use, Scrollingo may access:

- Microphone access, to support speech practice and pronunciation-related features.
- App usage and overlay-related permissions on Android, to support app blocking and focus features.
- Local app settings and progress stored on your device, such as selected languages, reward time, blocked apps, and lesson progress.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use this information only to operate the app's core features, including:

- Delivering pronunciation and speech-related learning features.
- Letting users configure and run app-blocking and focus workflows.
- Saving preferences and in-app progress locally on the device.

## 3. Microphone Permission

Scrollingo requests microphone permission because some learning features rely on spoken input.

At this stage of the product, microphone access is used only to support speech and pronunciation features inside the app. Users can deny this permission, but some speech-based features may not work correctly.

## 4. App Blocking Permissions

On Android, Scrollingo may request permissions such as overlay access, app usage access, and battery optimization exemption in order to:

- detect when a blocked app is opened,
- show a blocking screen or interruption layer,
- and keep focus-related features working reliably.

These permissions are used only for the app's blocking and focus functionality.

## 5. Data Storage

At the current MVP stage, Scrollingo does not rely on a user database for the core experience.

Most settings and progress are currently stored locally on the user's device. This may include:

- selected language pair,
- reward time configuration,
- blocked apps configuration,
- and local lesson or progress data.

## 6. Accounts and Sign-In

At the time of this policy update, Scrollingo may not require account creation or sign-in to use its main MVP features.

If account features, including Google sign-in or other authentication methods, are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or when those features are released.

## 7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

At the current MVP stage, Scrollingo is not intended to share personal information with third parties except where necessary to provide platform-level functionality, such as speech recognition or operating system services made available by Android, device manufacturers, or integrated libraries.

## 8. Children

Scrollingo is not directed specifically to children under the age required by applicable law in their jurisdiction. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and related app disclosures.

## 9. Security

We take reasonable steps to reduce unnecessary data collection and to keep the app focused on local-device functionality during the MVP stage. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as Scrollingo evolves, including when new features such as cloud sync, authentication, or additional integrations are introduced.

When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
